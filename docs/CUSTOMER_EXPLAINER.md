# How it works (plain-language — for future customers)

> Draft. This becomes onboarding and sales copy later. Written so a non-technical person gets it.

## What you get

A place to build websites and apps just by describing what you want. You type "make me a booking
page for my studio," and it builds it, shows you a live preview, and lets you change anything by
just asking. When it's ready, it goes live on the internet with one click.

## Why it's cheaper than the usual tools

Most AI builders charge you a monthly fee *and* mark up the AI usage on top. Here, you bring your
own AI key — you pay the AI company directly at their raw price, with no markup from us. We charge a
simple flat fee for setting the whole thing up and keeping it running for you. For most people that
works out much cheaper.

## What "bring your own key" means (and the only slightly techy part)

An "API key" is like a password that lets the builder use AI on your behalf, billed to you. You get
one from the AI provider (for example, Anthropic). It's a few steps — make an account, add a card,
copy a key — and **we walk you through it, step by step.** Once it's pasted in, you never think
about it again. That guided setup is the main thing we do for you.

## What you'll need (we help with all of it)

- An AI provider account + key (e.g., Anthropic) — you pay the AI usage
- A free GitHub account — where your project's code is safely stored
- A free hosting account (Vercel or Netlify) — where your finished live site runs

Everything above stays in *your* accounts, under your control. If you ever leave, you keep
everything.

## What we handle

The whole builder, hosted and ready. The setup. The "how do I get a key" hand-holding. Keeping it
running. You just build.
